Establishment

Chilli Pepper Robotics was founded by passionate students with a drive to compete at the highest levels of VEX Robotics. What started as a small group of builders and programmers has grown into a multi-city, award-winning organization spanning two countries.

Location

We operate across four cities in two countries — Ottawa and Markham, Ontario, Cary, North Carolina, and Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. Our teams train and compete locally while representing the Chilli Pepper name across North America.

Teams

This season, we field five competitive teams: 37X – Xtra Hot Chilli Peppers, 37S – Supa Hot Chilli Peppers, 2020Z – Double Take, 61568A – Genesis, and 3760V. Each team competes independently with its own identity, robot design, and strategy.

Diversity

Chilli Pepper Robotics isn't a single-school organization — it's a community. Our members come from many different schools, backgrounds, and cities. This diversity in perspective is one of our greatest strengths, fueling creative solutions and a richer team culture.

We pride ourselves on the mentorship, guidance, and advisory we provide one another — from build and programming tips to engineering notebook help. Experienced members actively coach newer ones, creating a culture where everyone levels up together.
