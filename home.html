01 — Who we are

A community of
builders and competitors.

Chilli Pepper Robotics is an elite, award-winning VEX organization dedicated to excellence in engineering, innovation, and competitive robotics. We proudly field five competitive teams across Canada and the United States.

Through a strong emphasis on collaboration, leadership, and real-world STEM application, our program develops skilled, forward-thinking students prepared to excel in competitive robotics — and beyond.
